Fusajiro Yamauchi

Founder of Nintendo, Fusajiro Yamauchi started the company in 1889 as a playing card manufacturer in Kyoto, Japan. His vision laid the groundwork for what would become a global leader in video game entertainment.

Consoles

While Fusajiro Yamauchi did not live to see the era of video game consoles, his company went on to create iconic systems like the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), Game Boy, Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES), and more.

  • Color TV-Game series (1977–1980)
  • Game & Watch series (1980–1991)
  • Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) / Famicom (1983)
  • Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) / Super Famicom (1990)
  • Virtual Boy (1995)
  • Nintendo 64 (1996)

Production

Nintendo's products are known for their quality and innovation, produced in state-of-the-art facilities around the world.
